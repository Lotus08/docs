---
title: 3rd party email providers
---

MagicBell can deliver email notifications for your users via 3rd party email providers like [Sendgrid](https://sendgrid.com/), [Postmark](https://postmarkapp.com/) and [Mailgun](https://www.mailgun.com/). Please [send us a note](mailto:hello@magicbell.io) to have it set up for your account.
